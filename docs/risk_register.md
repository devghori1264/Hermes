# Risk Register

1. External dependency volatility for third party content and metadata APIs
2. Model drift due to domain expansion and evolving user behavior
3. Latency regression from multi modal feature extraction and large models
4. Reproducibility risk without immutable experiment manifests and dataset hashes
5. Safety risk from unsupported generated explanations or inference about sensitive traits
6. Data rights risk from unclear licensing or missing attribution
7. Feedback loop risk that amplifies popularity bias and reduces catalog coverage
8. Cold start risk for new users and items without adequate content signals
9. Evaluation risk from offline metric misalignment with online outcomes
10. Operational risk from capacity exhaustion and dependency outage
11. Security risk from input abuse, prompt injection, or scraping blocks
12. Fairness risk across cohorts, locales, and long tail items
